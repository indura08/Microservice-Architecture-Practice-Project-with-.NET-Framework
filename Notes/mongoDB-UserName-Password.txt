If your password has special characters (@, #, $, %), they must be URL-encoded:

@ → %40

# → %23

$ → %24

Example: If your password is my@password, update it like:

mongodb+srv://username:my%40password@microservice-dotnet-pro.jmlcj4o.mongodb.net
